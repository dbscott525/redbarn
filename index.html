Our Story

Where Every Horse Matters

Located on the beautiful, quiet outskirts of Vacaville, Red Barn Rehab & Retirement is dedicated to providing exceptional care for horses in the greater Northern California area.

We specialize in offering a comprehensive range of services, including rehabilitation, layup boarding, and retirement — all tailored to meet the unique needs of each horse. We pride ourselves on creating a nurturing environment where horses can thrive, whether they are recovering from an injury or enjoying their golden years.

Red Barn is the ideal choice for the whole range of horse owners. Whether you want the best for your backyard pony or need to keep your top show horse at peak performance, we offer accessible treatments that benefit all.

Our Services

Most Popular

Rehabilitation

Comprehensive care and recovery solutions for horses of all ages and conditions, featuring state-of-the-art equipment and expert oversight.

  • Water Treadmill Low-impact exercise that improves joint flexibility and muscle strength — ideal for recovery from injuries and surgery
  • Cold Water Spa Reduces inflammation, swelling, and pain while promoting circulation and accelerating healing
  • Haul-In Day Treatments Don't need full boarding? Trailer your horse in for individual treatment sessions

Layup Boarding

Comprehensive and customizable care ensuring your horse's well-being and comfort during their recovery transition.

  • 24/7 On-Site Staff Constant supervision and attentive care around the clock
  • Flexible Housing Stalls with attached paddocks, or small and large outdoor paddocks with shelters
  • Seamless Transition Available to horses graduating from rehab or those needing dedicated support

Retirement

A compassionate approach to ensure the well-being and dignity of horses in their golden years.

  • Custom Feeding Plans Tailored nutrition promoting optimal health and vitality for each individual horse
  • Regular Veterinary Care Health monitoring and prompt attention to any medical concerns
  • Full Daily Care Feeding, grooming, exercise, and companionship — every horse is loved dearly
