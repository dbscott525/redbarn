Welcome

Where Every Horse Matters

Located on the beautiful, quiet outskirts of Vacaville, Red Barn Rehab & Retirement is dedicated to providing exceptional care for horses in the greater Northern California area.

We specialize in thoughtful, individualized care — including rehabilitation, layup boarding, and retirement — tailored to meet the unique needs of each horse. Our small facility allows us to focus on quality over quantity, ensuring every horse receives the attention they deserve.
