What We Offer

Our Services

Individualized care shaped around each horse's needs — offered based on fit and availability

Rehabilitation

Specialized recovery and wellness services currently in preparation, featuring professional-grade equipment and expert oversight.

Water Treadmill

Our water treadmill will offer a low-impact exercise option that is instrumental in aiding rehabilitation and recovery. This service is particularly beneficial for improving joint flexibility and muscle strength, making it an ideal choice for horses recovering from injuries, surgery, and other health problems. This service is currently being installed and will be available soon. Early inquiries are welcome.

Cold Water Spa

Our cold water spa is designed to be highly effective in reducing inflammation, swelling, and pain. It promotes circulation, healing, and shortens recovery times while strengthening muscles. Suitable for horses of all ages, sizes, and conditions, and particularly beneficial for aiding in wound healing. With short treatment times, this is typically a low-stress experience. This service is currently being installed and will be available soon. Early inquiries are welcome.

Haul-In Day Treatments

Once our rehabilitation equipment is fully installed, we plan to welcome haul-in clients for individual water treadmill and cold water spa sessions. Our intimate facility is well suited for this flexible option. If you're interested, we encourage you to reach out early.

Layup Boarding

Individualized and customizable care ensuring your horse's well-being and comfort during their recovery transition. Availability is limited and offered on a case-by-case basis.

24/7 On-Site Staff

Horses receive constant supervision and attentive care around the clock, providing peace of mind to their owners. Our dedicated team ensures your horse is monitored and cared for at all times.

Flexible Housing Options

Multiple housing options are available to suit the specific needs of each horse, ranging from stalls with attached paddocks to small and large outdoor paddocks with shelters. These features allow for a tailored boarding experience that prioritizes health, safety, and happiness.

Seamless Transition

Available to horses that have graduated from our rehabilitation program, making the shift to different activity and more space as effortless as possible. Also available to horses needing a facility to offer support for different reasons.

Retirement

A compassionate approach to ensure the well-being and dignity of horses in their golden years. We accept a small number of retirement horses to ensure each one receives the attention they deserve.

Custom Feeding Plans

Each horse receives a customized feeding plan tailored to its individual needs, promoting optimal health and vitality throughout their retirement years.

Regular Veterinary Care

Regular veterinary care and health monitoring allow prompt addressing of any medical concerns and maintain the horse's overall quality of life throughout their stay with us.

Full Daily Care

Our full care service encompasses all aspects of daily maintenance, from feeding and grooming to exercise and companionship. We ensure that every horse is well-cared for and loved dearly in their retirement.

Interested in Learning More?

Reach out to discuss your horse's needs and timing. Availability varies by service, and we're happy to help determine if Red Barn is the right fit.
