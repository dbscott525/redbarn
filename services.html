What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive care solutions designed around your horse's unique needs

Rehabilitation

Comprehensive care and recovery solutions for horses of all ages and conditions, featuring state-of-the-art equipment and expert oversight.

Water Treadmill

Our state-of-the-art water treadmill offers a low-impact exercise option that is instrumental in aiding rehabilitation and recovery. This service is particularly beneficial for improving joint flexibility and muscle strength, making it an ideal choice for horses recovering from injuries, surgery, and other health problems.

Cold Water Spa

Our cold water spa service is highly effective in reducing inflammation, swelling, and pain. It promotes circulation, healing, and shortens recovery times while strengthening muscles. Suitable for horses of all ages, sizes, and conditions, and particularly beneficial for aiding in wound healing. With short treatment times, this is typically a low-stress experience.

Haul-In Day Treatments

Don't need full boarding? Our flexible, intimate facility allows us to accommodate haul-in clients. Trailer your horse in for individual water treadmill or cold water spa sessions, giving us the chance to help an even greater number of horses without the constraint of open stall availability.

Layup Boarding

Comprehensive and customizable care ensuring your horse's well-being and comfort during their recovery transition.

24/7 On-Site Staff

Horses receive constant supervision and attentive care around the clock, providing peace of mind to their owners. Our dedicated team ensures your horse is monitored and cared for at all times.

Flexible Housing Options

Multiple housing options are available to suit the specific needs of each horse, ranging from stalls with attached paddocks to small and large outdoor paddocks with shelters. These features allow for a tailored boarding experience that prioritizes health, safety, and happiness.

Seamless Transition

Available to horses that have graduated from our rehabilitation program, making the shift to different activity and more space as effortless as possible. Also available to horses needing a facility to offer support for different reasons.

Retirement

A compassionate approach to ensure the well-being and dignity of horses in their golden years.

Custom Feeding Plans

Each horse receives a customized feeding plan tailored to its individual needs, promoting optimal health and vitality throughout their retirement years.

Regular Veterinary Care

Regular veterinary care and health monitoring allow prompt addressing of any medical concerns and maintain the horse's overall quality of life throughout their stay with us.

Full Daily Care

Our full care service encompasses all aspects of daily maintenance, from feeding and grooming to exercise and companionship. We ensure that every horse is well-cared for and loved dearly in their retirement.
