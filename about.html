Our Background

Before Red Barn Rehab & Retirement was officially created, the mission and operation on the property had similarities. We offered limited spots to horses of those we knew and loved for a variety of reasons.

Horses came to us to live out their golden years, take a mental and physical break from being under saddle, rehab from different types of injuries, and more. Our reputation has grown entirely through word-of-mouth — a testament to the quality of care we provide every single day.

Red Barn is the ideal choice for the whole range of horse owners. Whether you want the best for your backyard pony or need to keep your top show horse at peak performance, we offer accessible treatments that benefit all.
